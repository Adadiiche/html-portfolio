The Best Movies According to Vivian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Black Panther

An amazing Sci-Fi movie by marvel. Absolutely fantastic. Great cast and well selected scene.

Three Thousand Years of Longing

There are several movie stories about genie and their kinds but this was different and my all time favourite. It had a beautiful soundtrack and really played with my emotions.

Encanto

It is an animated musical by Disney about a large family blessed with magical gifts. It was really artistic and detailed.
